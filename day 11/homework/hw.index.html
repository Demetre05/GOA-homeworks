My top five films

1. The Revenant (2015)

  • Release date: December 25, 2015
  • IMDb rating: ⭐ 8.0 / 10

Set in the 1820s, the film follows frontiersman Hugh Glass, who is severely injured by a bear attack and left
for dead by his companions. Despite his wounds, he survives and journeys through the wilderness seeking revenge
on those who betrayed him.

2. John Wick (2014)

  • Release date: October 24, 2014
  • IMDb rating: ⭐ 7.4 / 10

After the death of his wife, a retired hitman named John Wick lives a quiet life until gangsters steal his car
and kill his dog—his last gift from his wife. This sparks his return to the criminal underworld for a violent revenge.

3. Gladiator (2000)

  • Release date: May 5, 2000
  • IMDb rating: ⭐ 8.5 / 10

Maximus, a Roman general, is betrayed by the emperor's son, Commodus, who murders his family and seizes power.
Sold into slavery, Maximus becomes a gladiator and fights his way through the arena seeking vengeance and justice.

4. Forrest Gump (1994)

  • Release date: July 6, 1994
  • IMDb rating: ⭐ 8.8 / 10

The story follows Forrest Gump, a kind-hearted man with a low IQ, as he unwittingly influences major historical
events in America. Despite his challenges, his innocence and love for Jenny lead him through a remarkable life
filled with surprises.

5. The Green Mile (1999)

  • Release date: December 10, 1999
  • IMDb rating: ⭐ 8.6 / 10

Set in a 1930s prison, the story centers on Paul Edgecomb, a death row guard who meets John Coffey, a gentle giant
with miraculous healing powers. As strange events unfold, Paul realizes that Coffey may be innocent — and even divine.
